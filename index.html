Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to show results?
+

SEO generally starts showing measurable improvements within 3–6 months depending on competition and industry.

What is GEO optimization?
+

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) helps businesses appear in AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.

What is AEO optimization?
+

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content to appear in direct answers, featured snippets, and voice searches.
